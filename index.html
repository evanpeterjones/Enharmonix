Where we began...

In 2011, two Appalachian State students came together to create Enharmonix - the goal being to strive for musical excellence with a mind toward diversity and acceptance...


Where we are now...

Today, ENX has won awards for "Best Overall Group" and "Best Co-Ed Group" at Appalachian State's Acappellageddon consecutive years as well as opening for Us The Duo in concert, competing in the semi-finals for the 2017 & 2018 ICCA's, and receiving awards for "Outstanding Composition", "Outstanding Beatboxer", and "Outstanding Vocalist"...


Where we're headed...

Outside of competition and performance we pride ourselves in having fostered meaningful relationships with many of the clubs and organizations at Appalachian and in the broader acapella community alike.

We've performed at competitions, charity events, weddings, and various events on college campuses. So, if you're interested in having us perform for you, feel free to send us an email.

We continue to set new goals and are looking forward to see what we can do next...
