Enharmonix

Where it all started...

In 2011, over a meal at Bojangles', two Appalachian State students created Enharmonix. The goal of this group was to strive for musical excellence while orienting themselves toward diversity and acceptance.

Today, ENX is proud to say we have accomplished the goals of our founders. Winning Best Overall Group and Best Co-Ed Group at Appalachian State's Acappellageddon consecutive years and going on to compete in the semi-finals for the ICCA's last year. Outside of competition and performance we pride ourselves in having fostered a meaningful relationship with many of the clubs and organizations at Appalachian and in the community alike.

We continue to set new goals and are looking forward to see what comes next.

We've been invited across state lines to perform for competitions, charity events, Weddings, and even for various events on college campuses. If you're interested in having us perform for you, please use this contact form. Thank you.
